Comparing IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes

IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes, are two of the most popular online movie rating websites today, each with their own approach to movie ratings. Between the two there are five main metrics used which are summarized below.

Five Rating Metrics:

  • Tomatometer: The percentage of "Tomatometer Approved Critics" who gave the movie a positive review.
  • Critic Average: The average rating given by critics to the movie.
  • Audience Score: The percentage of RT users who rated the movie 3.5/5 or higher.
  • Audience Average: The average rating given by users to the movie.
  • IMDb Rating: The weighted average rating given to the movie by users.
